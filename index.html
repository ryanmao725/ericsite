About

When I was little, I couldn't really decide on what I wanted to be when I grew up. Then, in 6th grade, I learned about the Renaissance. I finally decided that what I wanted to be was a "Renaissance man." someone who is adept at many different skills and fields of knowledge.

This site is representative of the many interests that I have, and it also shows the things I've worked on. I've dabbled in a lot of areas, and this site is also a way for me to keep track and try to finish all the things I've started.

But what remains consistent is that: I'm a creative, passionate, curious individual who values honesty on top of all other values. I'm always interested in how things work, and I enjoy learning about other perspectives and approaches to issues.

Some topics that I am currently interested in:

  • Healthcare
  • Advertising
  • Consumer Technology
  • Transportation/Logistics
  • Alternative Energy
  • Initiatives that make the world a better place
